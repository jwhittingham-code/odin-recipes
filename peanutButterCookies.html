Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

My special recipe makes the peanut butteriest tasting cookie I have ever tasted. These soft and chewy peanut buttery cookies are the best!


Ingredients

  • 1cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the peanut butter,butter,sugars until blended well
  3. Beat in egg, milk and vanilla, one at a time
  4. Combine flour, baking powder and salt; stir into creamed mixture
  5. Roll dough into small balls, place 5cm apart on baking tray and press each with a fork
  6. Bake for ten minutes
