Blueberry Dutch Baby

Description

You're in for a huge treat with this blueberry studded baked pancake--it's rich and satisfying but surprisingly light tasting. The true magic of a Dutch baby is the contrast of texture between the crispy, crusty outside and the soft, custardy, fruity center.
This version is not only gorgeous and delicious, but it's fun and simple to make! There's no sugar in the batter, so all the sweetness comes from the berries and the powdered sugar. For a sweeter version, serve with maple syrup like regular blueberry pancakes.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup plain flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/3 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. combine flour, salt,vanilla, eggs and milk in a high speed blender for about 1 minute
  3. heat a cast iron skillet over high heat. Pour in 3 tablespoons of butter, heat till starting to smoke
  4. Quickly pour in batter, sprinkle blueberries on top. transfer skillet to oven and bake for 20-25 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and brush with remaining butter. squeeze lemon juice over the top and dust with sugar
  6. cut and serve with a drizzle of maple syrup
